> Nutrition score is based on how well a given food contributes to my health goals.
> 1 - The food is highly processed and/or has high sugars and/or fats with little protein or fiber.
> 2 - The food is moderately processed and/or has moderately high sugars and/or fats with low amounts of protein or fiber.
> 3 - The food is mostly nutritionally neutral; there are not significant amounts of sugars and/or fat, but it also offers little benefit with protein or fiber; OR the food has decent protein or fiber but also a moderate amount of sugars or fat.
> 4 - The food is minimally processed and/or has low sugars and/or fats with moderate protein or fiber.
> 5 - The food is a whole or mostly whole food with low sugars and/or fats with high protein or fiber.
